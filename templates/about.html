Our Story

A Machine Learning Academic Project

Insurify is a machine learning project developed in 2026 by students of Sanjay Ghodawat University. It focuses on predicting medical insurance costs based on factors like age, BMI, and lifestyle, helping users understand insurance pricing more clearly.
